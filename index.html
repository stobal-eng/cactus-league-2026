1 Reds @ Giants

Scottsdale Stadium
Sec 306 Row 5 Seats 3-5 · Row 6 Seats 3-4
🅿️
Parking Cost
Cashless
No dedicated stadium lot. Nearby public lots $10 – $20
📍
Where to Park
Scottsdale Civic Center Library garage just south of the stadium is the go-to. Additional paid lots along Drinkwater Blvd. Arrive early — Old Town lots fill fast.
👜
Bag Policy
Clear Bag Required
Clear totes up to 12" x 12" x 6" or small clutch purses up to 9" x 5". No backpacks — including clear backpacks.
☀️
Sun / Shade
Full Shade🌤 ~68°F at first pitch
Section 306 is covered by the stadium canopy. Rows 3 and up are shaded for the entire game — you're in Row 5-6, so you'll be completely in the shade from first pitch.
⭐
Don't Miss
The stadium is in the heart of Old Town Scottsdale — the best walkable location of any Cactus League park. Post-game, show your ticket stub at nearby bars for deals: $2 Modelo at The Montauk, $3 beers at El Camino, or 20% off at Estelle. Pizzeria Virtù (steps away) has grab-and-go folded Neapolitan pizza.

2 Cubs @ Padres

Peoria Sports Complex
Sec 115 Row M Seats 4-8
🅿️
Parking Cost
Free
Free parking for all spring training games
📍
Where to Park
On-site surface lots. Enter from Mariners Way or Stadium Way off 83rd Ave.
👜
Bag Policy
Clear Bag Required
Clear totes up to 12" x 12" x 6". Small clutch/wallet up to 4.5" x 6.5" must be inside your clear bag. No purses, bags, or backpacks.
☀️
Sun / Shade
Night Game🌙 ~60°F at first pitch
6:10 PM start — the sun will be low and setting by first pitch in early March. Sec 115 is lower-level (no built-in shade cover), but it won't matter. Temps drop fast after sunset — bring a light layer.
⭐
Don't Miss
Known for having the best variety of food among the smaller parks — gyros, noodle bowls, churros, and great craft beer. Hit Autograph Alley on the right field concourse early to snag player signatures. Tailgating is allowed in the parking lot before games.

3 A's @ Cubs

Sloan Park · Mesa
Sec 101 Row 16 Seats 1-5
🅿️
Parking Cost
Cashless
$10 reserved  ·  $5 general
📍
Where to Park
On-site lots east of the ballpark off Rio Salado Pkwy. First-come, first-served. Lots open 4 hrs before game. Credit card only — no cash.
👜
Bag Policy
Bags OK
Backpacks, purses, and drawstring bags allowed up to 16" x 16" x 8". No clear bag requirement. Soft-sided coolers permitted.
☀️
Sun / Shade
Mostly Shade🌤 ~75°F at first pitch
Almost all grandstand seats at Sloan Park are shaded. Sec 101 is on the third-base side. At Row 16, you should be under shade for most of the game — rows 8+ on the third-base side get shade shortly after the 1:05 start.
⭐
Don't Miss
Dubbed "Wrigleyville West" — the whole park is a mini Wrigley Field replica with matching outfield curves, an analog clock, and ivy-covered walls. Get a photo at the replica Wrigley marquee (they'll put your name on it for free). Best food in the Cactus League: Vienna Beef Chicago dogs, Giordano's stuffed pizza, and rotating food trucks in the right field pavilion. Cubs practice fields are open to the public pre-game.

4 Rockies @ Dodgers

Camelback Ranch · Glendale
Sec 28 Row 6 Seats 5-6 · Row 7 Seats 4-6
🅿️
Parking Cost
Free
Free parking for all spring training games
📍
Where to Park
Large on-site lots off Camelback Rd & 111th Ave. You park on the far side and walk through the practice fields to the stadium. No neighborhood parking (tow enforced).
👜
Bag Policy
Clear Bag Required
Clear bags up to 12" x 12" x 6" or clutch up to 5" x 9". No backpacks. $20 bag storage available outside the home plate ticket office if you bring the wrong bag.
☀️
Sun / Shade
Night Game🌙 ~66°F at first pitch
6:05 PM start — sun will be setting behind the third-base grandstand. Sec 28 is the Dodgers dugout side (third-base), which normally gets heavy sun at day games, but you'll dodge it with this evening start. Bring a hoodie — desert temps can swing 20°+ after sunset.
⭐
Don't Miss
The walk from the parking lot through the practice fields is part of the experience — you'll pass Dodgers and White Sox players warming up on 12 practice diamonds connected by paths and a scenic lake. Grab a Dodger Dog or a Chicago Dog (both available at every game). The 141-acre complex is one of the most beautiful in spring training. The Westgate Entertainment District is a short drive north for post-game food and drinks.

5 Dodgers @ A's

Hohokam Stadium · Mesa
Sec 101 Row 8 Seats 3-7
🅿️
Parking Cost
Cash Only
$5 per vehicle
📍
Where to Park
On-site lot on the grass fields north & south of the stadium off Center St. Bring cash — no cards accepted for parking.
👜
Bag Policy
Bags OK
Bags up to 16" x 16" x 8" allowed. No clear bag requirement — any bag type is fine as long as it's under the size limit.
☀️
Sun / Shade
Partial Sun🌤 ~83°F at first pitch
Sec 101 is on the lower level near the infield. The grandstand canopy mostly covers the 200-level terrace seats. At Row 8 you'll likely have sun early, with shade arriving by mid-game as the shadows extend. Bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat for the first few innings.
⭐
Don't Miss
A small, intimate park with a classic minor-league feel — you're close enough to chat with players. Try the Mustache Pretzels food truck on the main concourse and Ike's Love & Sandwiches (SF-based, some of the best food in spring training). The Stomper's Kid Zone has t-ball and skeeball for kids 12 and under. Hohokam has the largest LED scoreboard in the Cactus League. The right field deck has open drink-rail seating with a great view.
